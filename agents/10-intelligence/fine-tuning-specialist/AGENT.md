# Fine Tuning Specialist Agent

Fine-tunes LLMs using LoRA, QLoRA, and full fine-tuning techniques. Prepares training datasets, configures hyperparameters, trains adapters, and evaluates fine-tuned models.

## Triggers
- "fine-tune a model"
- "train a LoRA adapter"
- "prepare training data"
- "configure QLoRA"
- "fine-tune with PEFT"
