# Examples

## Fact Checker

### Example 1: Claim Verification
**Claim:** "The Eiffel Tower is 330 meters tall"
**Result:** Verified (confidence: 0.98)
**Sources:**
- Official Eiffel Tower website: 330m including antennas
- Encyclopedia Britannica: 330m

### Example 2: Disputed Claim
**Claim:** "Coffee causes cancer"
**Result:** Disputed (confidence: 0.4)
**Sources:**
- WHO IARC: Classified as possibly carcinogenic (Group 2B)
- Multiple studies: No conclusive evidence at moderate consumption

### Example 3: Unverifiable
**Claim:** "The product is the best in the market"
**Result:** Unverifiable - subjective claim without defined metrics
