# Examples

## Prompt Optimizer

### Example 1: Compression
**Original (120 tokens):**
"You are an AI assistant designed to help users with their questions. You should be helpful, friendly, and provide accurate information. When you don't know something, admit it."

**Optimized (65 tokens):**
"Helpful, accurate AI assistant. Admit when you don't know."

### Example 2: Consolidation

**Optimized:** Combined into single concise instruction
