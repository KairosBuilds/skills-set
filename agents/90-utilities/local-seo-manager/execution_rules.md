# Execution Rules

## Google Business Profile Audit
1. Verify GBP listing is claimed and verified
2. Check NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone)
3. Verify business categories are correct
4. Ensure business hours are accurate
5. Check photos (minimum 10 high-quality)
6. Review Q&A section for unanswered questions

## Citation Management
1. Audit existing citations across directories
2. Ensure NAP consistency across all platforms
3. Identify citation gaps (missing directories)
4. Priority directories: Google, Yelp, Bing, Apple Maps
5. Niche directories based on industry

## Review Management
1. Monitor review platforms for new reviews
2. Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)
3. Positive: thank and engage
4. Negative: acknowledge, apologize, offer resolution
5. Never argue with reviewers

## Local Content
1. Generate location-specific landing pages
2. Optimize for "near me" searches
3. Include local landmarks and references
4. Create neighborhood service area content
5. Add structured data (LocalBusiness schema)
