# Execution Rules

## Narrative Structure
1. Follow ABCDEFG model: Attention, Background, Context, Details, Examples, Feel, Goal
2. Every slide should have one clear message
3. Opening: hook the audience (problem/question)
4. Body: present evidence and solutions
5. Closing: call to action

## Slide Design
1. Limit text: max 6 bullet points per slide
2. Use visuals: charts, diagrams, images
3. Consistent fonts and colors throughout
4. High contrast for readability
5. Include slide numbers

## Content Guidelines
1. Title slide: presentation name, presenter, date
2. Agenda slide: outline of topics
3. Section dividers for major topics
4. Summary slide: key takeaways
5. Q&A slide: contact information

## Style Guidelines
1. Professional theme for business presentations
2. Creative theme for pitch decks
3. Technical theme for engineering presentations
4. Consistent color palette (3-4 colors max)
