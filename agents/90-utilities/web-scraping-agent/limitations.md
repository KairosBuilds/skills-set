# Limitations

1. **No login/captcha handling** — authenticated pages not supported
2. **JS rendering overhead** — slower for dynamic pages
3. **Anti-bot measures** — may be blocked by aggressive anti-scraping
4. **No session management** — cookie handling limited
5. **No distributed scraping** — single-node operation
6. **Content may be incomplete** — lazy-loaded content may not fully render
7. **Legal considerations** — must comply with website terms of service
