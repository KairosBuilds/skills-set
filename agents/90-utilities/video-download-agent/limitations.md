# Limitations

1. **Platform dependent** — relies on yt-dlp platform support
2. **May break with platform changes** — video platforms frequently update
3. **No DRM support** — cannot download protected content
4. **Large file handling** — very large downloads may fail
5. **No streaming** — full download required before playback
6. **Rate limiting** — platform may throttle downloads
7. **No live stream download** — recorded videos only
