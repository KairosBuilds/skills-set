# Limitations

1. **Availability data may be stale** — WHOIS may not be real-time
2. **Cannot register domains** — lookup only
3. **No trademark legal advice** — cannot verify trademark status
4. **Limited TLD coverage** — focuses on popular TLDs
5. **No domain valuation** — cannot estimate market value
6. **No auction/sale listings** — cannot find expiring domains
7. **Premium domain detection limited** — may not identify all premium domains
