# Workflow

1. **Gather Inputs**: Business goals, resource capacity, market data
2. **Define Initiatives**: High-level epics aligned to objectives
3. **Set Milestones**: Key delivery points with dates
4. **Assign Confidence**: Score each initiative (H/M/L)
5. **Map Dependencies**: Cross-initiative and external dependencies
6. **Allocate Resources**: Match teams to initiatives
7. **Review**: Quarterly review cycle with stakeholders
8. **Output**: Structured roadmap with timeline and confidence levels
