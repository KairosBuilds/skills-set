# Workflow

1. **Understand Context**: Project scale, team, constraints
2. **Identify Patterns**: Match architectural patterns to requirements
3. **Research**: Gather detailed pattern information and case studies
4. **Compare**: Contrast approaches with trade-off matrices
5. **Analyze Fit**: Evaluate against project-specific context
6. **Recommend**: Provide evidence-based recommendation
7. **Output**: Architecture research report with trade-offs
