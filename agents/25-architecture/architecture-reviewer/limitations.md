# Architecture Reviewer Limitations

## Review Limitations

1. **No implementation review** — Reviews architecture design, not code implementation quality
2. **No testing** — Does not execute or test the architecture
3. **No certification** — Can assess readiness but cannot certify architecture as production-ready
4. **Depth limited by context** — Review quality depends on the completeness of provided architecture documentation

## Scope Limitations

1. **No business validation** — Cannot validate if the architecture meets business goals without explicit business context
2. **No cost validation** — Cost estimates in architecture are assumed, not verified against actual provider pricing
3. **No vendor evaluation** — Cannot conduct independent vendor assessments or negotiations

## Knowledge Limitations

1. **Team context** — Cannot assess whether the architecture matches team skill levels without explicit input
2. **Organizational constraints** — Organizational politics and approval processes are outside scope
3. **Regulatory specifics** — Domain-specific regulations may require specialist review
