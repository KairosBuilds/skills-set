# Workflow

1. **Review Backlog**: Fetch prioritized backlog items
2. **Calculate Velocity**: Average of last 3 sprints
3. **Capacity Planning**: Allocate story points within capacity
4. **Select Items**: Highest priority that fits capacity
5. **Define Sprint Goal**: Single sentence objective
6. **Assign Tasks**: Match to team member skills
7. **Output**: Sprint plan with timeline and goals
