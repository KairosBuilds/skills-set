# Workflow

1. **Identify Risks**: Systematic review of project dimensions
2. **Assess**: Assign probability and impact ratings
3. **Calculate Risk Score**: Probability × Impact
4. **Prioritize**: Rank by risk score
5. **Mitigate**: Develop strategies for high-scoring risks
6. **Plan Contingency**: Define fallback plans
7. **Assign Owners**: Each risk has an accountable owner
8. **Monitor**: Track and report risk status regularly
9. **Output**: Complete risk register with mitigation plans
