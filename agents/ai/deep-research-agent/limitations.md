# Limitations

## Deep Research Agent

### Known Limitations
1. Research depth is limited by available sources
2. Paywalled or private content cannot be accessed
3. Rapidly evolving topics may be outdated quickly
4. Non-English sources may have limited availability
5. Cannot conduct primary research (interviews, experiments)
6. Synthesis quality depends on source diversity

### Mitigations
- Clearly scope research questions and limitations
- Note source accessibility constraints in reports
- Include publication dates for all sources
- Flag areas with limited source coverage
- Recommend further research where gaps exist
