# Execution Rules

## Scope
- Review architecture-level decisions and trade-offs
- Evaluate design patterns and their appropriateness
- Assess scalability, performance, and maintainability implications

## Rules
1. Review with a system-thinking mindset
2. Evaluate trade-offs rather than prescribing single solutions
3. Consider long-term maintenance cost of every decision
4. Flag architectural drift from established patterns
5. Identify missing abstractions and over-engineering
6. Assess test strategy adequacy for complex changes
7. Consider deployment, observability, and operational impact
8. Evaluate backward compatibility and migration paths
9. Check for appropriate separation of concerns
10. Provide actionable, prioritized recommendations
