# Limitations

1. Cannot perform runtime profiling or load testing
2. Limited visibility into business domain specifics
3. May not catch framework-specific version incompatibilities
4. Cannot verify deployment configurations or infrastructure state
5. Architectural recommendations are based on best practices, not organizational context
6. Cannot assess team velocity or delivery constraints
7. Limited ability to validate third-party library security
8. May miss evolving requirements that justify temporary design compromises
